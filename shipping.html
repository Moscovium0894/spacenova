Shipping Information

All orders are printed and dispatched from our UK studio within 3 working days.

Delivery Options

Dispatch Time

Every order is printed to order and dispatched within 3 working days. You will receive a dispatch email with your tracking number as soon as your parcel leaves our studio.

Packaging

Plates are individually wrapped in acid-free tissue and packed in a rigid double-walled box. We use 100% recycled and recyclable packaging materials throughout.

Tracking

All orders include tracked shipping as standard. Your tracking link will be emailed to you when your order is dispatched and can also be found in your order confirmation.

Duties & Taxes

UK orders include VAT. International customers may be subject to import duties and taxes on arrival — these are the responsibility of the recipient and vary by country.
