Our Story

Born from a love of the cosmos

Spacenova was founded on a simple idea: the most awe-inspiring images ever taken deserve to live on your walls, not buried in a NASA archive.

The Mission

Art as vast as space itself

Every Spacenova panel begins with a genuine deep-space photograph — captured by observatories, space telescopes, and ISS crews. We work with astrophotographers worldwide to select only images that convey the true scale and beauty of the universe.

Our hexagonal magnetic system means no drilling, no commitment. Arrange a single statement piece or build an entire galaxy wall — then change it whenever inspiration strikes.

✦

Authentic Photography

Every image is a real photograph of the universe — no CGI, no composites. Just the cosmos as it truly is.

✦

Museum-Quality Print

Printed on archival-grade materials with pigment inks rated for over 100 years. Colours that last a lifetime.

✦

Magnetic System

Our patent-pending hexagonal magnetic mounting system installs in minutes and leaves no damage on your walls.

✦

UK Made

Printed and assembled in the United Kingdom. Every order ships within 3 working days.
