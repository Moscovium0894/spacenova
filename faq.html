Products

Our panels are made from a rigid composite board with an archival-quality print surface. The magnetic backing system uses industrial-grade rare earth magnets rated for thousands of attachment cycles.

Standard panels measure 30cm point-to-point. Large panels measure 45cm point-to-point. Both sizes are compatible with the same magnetic mounting system and can be mixed freely.

Yes. Every image is a genuine photograph taken by an observatory, space telescope, or ISS crew. We do not use CGI or composites. Colours may be enhanced to bring out scientifically accurate spectral data invisible to the naked eye.

Our pigment inks and archival substrate are rated for over 100 years when kept out of direct sunlight. UV-resistant coating is applied to every panel as standard.

Installation

No tools required. The magnetic wall plates attach with a peel-and-stick adhesive rated for smooth and painted surfaces. No drilling, no damage.

Our adhesive is specifically formulated to remove cleanly from painted plaster, plasterboard, and most tile surfaces. We recommend testing on a small area first if you have delicate or wallpapered walls.
