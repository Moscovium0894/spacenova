Our Story

Born from a love of the cosmos

We believe the universe's most breathtaking vistas deserve a permanent place in your home — printed with precision, designed with obsession.

The Mission

Spacenova began with a simple idea: the Hubble Space Telescope had gifted humanity images of incomprehensible beauty, yet they existed only as JPEG files on NASA servers. We set out to change that — to translate cosmic photography into objects worthy of the spaces we live in.

The Technology

Each panel uses a precision-cut hexagonal frame with an integrated magnetic rear. Panels connect to each other and to a hidden wall mounting system with no visible hardware. The prints themselves are produced on museum-grade archival stock with pigment inks rated for over 100 years.

The Photography

Our imagery is sourced from the world's leading space observatories — NASA, ESA, and the Hubble Heritage Team — processed by our in-house team to achieve print-perfect colour fidelity while preserving every scientific detail captured in the original data.
