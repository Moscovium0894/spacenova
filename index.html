Our story

Crafted for the curious

SPACENOVA was born from a simple obsession: making the cosmos feel tangible. Every panel is gallery-printed on aluminium, precision-cut, and fitted with rare-earth magnets so your mural snaps together perfectly and rearranges effortlessly.

Gallery aluminium print

Ultra-vivid, anti-glare surface. Colours that endure for decades without fading.

Magnetic alignment system

Rare-earth magnets embedded in every edge. Panels click into place within a millimetre.

Precision hexagon cut

CNC-routed to ±0.2 mm tolerance. Every tiling is seamless.

Delivery information

Shipping

Every Spacenova order is hand-checked and packed with care before it leaves our UK facility.

🇬🇧

UK Standard

Free on all orders. Tracked delivery in 3–5 working days via Royal Mail Tracked 48.

⚡

UK Express

£9.95. Next working day if ordered before 1 pm. Tracked and signed for.

🌍

Europe

£14.95 flat rate. 5–8 working days via DHL Economy. Fully tracked.

✈️

International

£19.95 flat rate. 7–14 working days. Includes US, Canada, Australia, and more.

Made to order

All Spacenova panels are printed fresh for each order — we don't hold stock. Please allow 5–7 working days production time before despatch. You'll receive a despatch email with tracking the moment your order ships.

Packaging

Panels are individually wrapped in archival tissue, layered in bespoke foam insets, and shipped in rigid double-walled cardboard. The outer box is unbranded for security. All materials are recyclable.

Customs & duties

International orders may be subject to local import duties and taxes. These are the responsibility of the buyer and are not included in our shipping rates. We declare accurate values on all customs forms.

Peace of mind

Returns & Exchanges

We want you to love what's on your wall. If anything isn't right, we'll make it right — no fuss.

30-day happiness guarantee

If you're not completely satisfied, return your order within 30 days of delivery for a full refund — including the original shipping cost.

How to start a return

1

Contact us

Email returns@spacenova.co.uk with your order reference and reason. We'll respond within 1 working day with a prepaid return label.

2

Repack carefully

Use the original packaging where possible. Panels should be wrapped individually. If you've discarded packaging, use equivalent rigid protection.

3

Drop off or arrange collection

Drop at any Royal Mail post office using the prepaid label, or request a free home collection for orders over £100.

4

Refund processed

Once received and inspected (usually within 2 working days), your refund is issued to the original payment method within 3–5 business days.

Damaged or faulty items

If a panel arrives damaged, please photograph it within 48 hours and email us. We'll despatch a replacement immediately at no cost — no need to return the damaged item.

Bespoke & custom orders

Custom-configured panel sets cannot be returned unless faulty, as they are made specifically for you. We'll always show a digital proof for approval before production.
