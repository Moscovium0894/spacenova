Our story

Crafted for the curious

SPACENOVA was born from a simple obsession: making the cosmos feel tangible. Every panel is gallery-printed on aluminium, precision-cut, and fitted with rare-earth magnets so your mural snaps together perfectly.

🖼️

Gallery aluminium print

Ultra-vivid, anti-glare surface. Colours that endure for decades.

🧲

Magnetic alignment system

Rare-earth magnets embedded in every edge. Click-together precision.

⬡

Precision hexagon cut

CNC-routed to ±0.2mm tolerance. Every tiling is seamless.

Europe

Packaging

How to start a return

1

Contact us

Email returns@spacenova.co.uk with your order reference. We respond within 1 working day with a prepaid return label.

2

Repack carefully

Use original packaging where possible. Panels should be individually wrapped.

3

Drop off or arrange collection

Drop at any Royal Mail post office, or request free home collection on orders over £100.

4

Refund processed

Inspected within 2 working days. Refund to original payment method in 3–5 business days.

Photograph within 48 hours and email us. We despatch a replacement immediately at no cost.
